Enabling Burst Transactions on an AXIM Interface in HLS

2021-06-01 :: Ramon Heras
#Xilinx-HLS  #AXI-Master  #C++  #Burst-Transactions 
Burst transactions are essential to take full advantage of the AXIM interface. After performing many tests and reading the documentation several times, I must say that there is not a well-defined behavior of Vitis HLS. In this post, I summarize my thoughts and experimentation results on optimizing burst transactions. You can find an introductory blog to this topic here. Unfortunately, the only way to ensure bursting is by diving into the wave viewer to check for it yourself.
